# Executive Summary — Bond Risk Lab

Bond Risk Lab is a fixed-income analytics and training platform for duration-convexity sensitivity analysis. It evaluates a diversified bond portfolio under parallel, slope and curvature yield-curve shocks, estimates duration and convexity P&L, computes DV01/PVBP, runs Monte Carlo VaR and CVaR, and demonstrates ML-based bond price prediction.

The premium Streamlit interface is designed as an analyst control tower with KPI cards, scenario controls, yield-curve visualisations, stress decomposition, and a gamified decision lab for fixed-income risk training.
